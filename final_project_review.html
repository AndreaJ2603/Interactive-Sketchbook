Final Project Review

It is that the human mind naturally gravitates toward the path of least effort, likely by design. I think life's complexity has trained our brains to simply take shortcuts. With technology being still new to the world, many people assume devices will be difficult to use—even when instructions are clear—and still hesitate or second-guess them.

The minute I encounter a bump or glitch in an interaction at least for myself, it will destroy the whole experience ahead with that device. From that moment on all I will look up is for the flaws on that system until I am proved right so I can move to something better!!
